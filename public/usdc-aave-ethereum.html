About USDC Aave

USDC Aave is a autocompounder vault on Ethereum that accepts USDC deposits and routes them into the Aave - Ethereum strategy. The vault automatically harvests rewards, swaps them back into USDC and redeposits, compounding returns over time without manual harvesting or restaking.

The vault currently holds $51 in deposits and is generating 5.33% APY over the last 24 hours. The 30-day average APY sits at 5.33%, and over its lifetime share price has grown 2.92% since inception.
