About USDC Hyperithm

USDC Hyperithm is a autocompounder vault on Arbitrum that accepts USDC deposits and routes them into the Morpho - Arbitrum strategy. The vault automatically harvests rewards, swaps them back into USDC and redeposits, compounding returns over time without manual harvesting or restaking.

The vault currently holds $27 in deposits and is generating 3.75% APY over the last 24 hours. The 30-day average APY sits at 3.75%, and over its lifetime share price has grown 170.65% since inception.
