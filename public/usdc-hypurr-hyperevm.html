About USDC Hypurr

USDC Hypurr is a autocompounder vault on HyperEVM that accepts USDC deposits and routes them into the Hypurr strategy. The vault automatically harvests rewards, swaps them back into USDC and redeposits, compounding returns over time without manual harvesting or restaking.
