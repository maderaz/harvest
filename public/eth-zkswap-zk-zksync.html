About ETH zkSwap

ETH zkSwap is a autocompounder vault on zkSync that accepts ETH deposits and routes them into the zkSwap strategy. The vault automatically harvests rewards, swaps them back into ETH and redeposits, compounding returns over time without manual harvesting or restaking.
