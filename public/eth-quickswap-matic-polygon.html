About ETH Quickswap

ETH Quickswap is a autocompounder vault on Polygon that accepts ETH deposits and routes them into the Quickswap strategy. The vault automatically harvests rewards, swaps them back into ETH and redeposits, compounding returns over time without manual harvesting or restaking.
