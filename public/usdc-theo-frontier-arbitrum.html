About USDC Theo Frontier

USDC Theo Frontier is a autocompounder vault on Arbitrum that accepts USDC deposits and routes them into the Euler - Arbitrum strategy. The vault automatically harvests rewards, swaps them back into USDC and redeposits, compounding returns over time without manual harvesting or restaking.
