About ETH Aerodrome

ETH Aerodrome is a autocompounder vault on Base that accepts ETH deposits and routes them into the Aerodrome - Base strategy. The vault automatically harvests rewards, swaps them back into ETH and redeposits, compounding returns over time without manual harvesting or restaking.

The vault currently holds $52K in deposits and is generating 19.17% APY over the last 24 hours. The 30-day average APY sits at 17.59%, and over its lifetime share price has grown 38.49% since inception.
