About USDC Lend

USDC Lend is a autocompounder vault on Base that accepts USDC deposits and routes them into the Arcadia - Base strategy. The vault automatically harvests rewards, swaps them back into USDC and redeposits, compounding returns over time without manual harvesting or restaking.

The vault currently holds $81K in deposits and is generating 5.84% APY over the last 24 hours. The 30-day average APY sits at 7.28%, and over its lifetime share price has grown 7.36% since inception.
