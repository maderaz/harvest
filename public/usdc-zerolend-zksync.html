About USDC ZeroLend

USDC ZeroLend is a autocompounder vault on zkSync that accepts USDC deposits and routes them into the ZeroLend strategy. The vault automatically harvests rewards, swaps them back into USDC and redeposits, compounding returns over time without manual harvesting or restaking.
