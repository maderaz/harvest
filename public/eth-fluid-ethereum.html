About ETH Fluid

ETH Fluid is a autocompounder vault on Ethereum that accepts ETH deposits and routes them into the Fluid strategy. The vault automatically harvests rewards, swaps them back into ETH and redeposits, compounding returns over time without manual harvesting or restaking.

The vault currently holds $95 in deposits and is generating 1.55% APY over the last 24 hours. The 30-day average APY sits at 1.55%, and over its lifetime share price has grown 2.82% since inception.
