About USDC Dolomite

USDC Dolomite is a autocompounder vault on Arbitrum that accepts USDC deposits and routes them into the Dolomite - Arbitrum strategy. The vault automatically harvests rewards, swaps them back into USDC and redeposits, compounding returns over time without manual harvesting or restaking.

The vault currently holds $33K in deposits and is generating 6.48% APY over the last 24 hours. The 30-day average APY sits at 5.78%, and over its lifetime share price has grown 10.04% since inception.
