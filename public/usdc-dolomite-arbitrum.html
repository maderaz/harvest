About USDC Dolomite

USDC Dolomite is a autocompounder vault on Arbitrum that accepts USDC deposits and routes them into the Dolomite strategy. The vault automatically harvests rewards, swaps them back into USDC and redeposits, compounding returns over time without manual harvesting or restaking.
