About WBTC Quickswap

WBTC Quickswap is a autocompounder vault on Polygon that accepts BTC deposits and routes them into the Quickswap strategy. The vault automatically harvests rewards, swaps them back into BTC and redeposits, compounding returns over time without manual harvesting or restaking.
