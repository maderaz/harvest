About ETH Dolomite

ETH Dolomite is a autocompounder vault on Arbitrum that accepts ETH deposits and routes them into the Dolomite - Arbitrum strategy. The vault automatically harvests rewards, swaps them back into ETH and redeposits, compounding returns over time without manual harvesting or restaking.

The vault currently holds $31K in deposits and is generating 2.61% APY over the last 24 hours. The 30-day average APY sits at 1.62%, and over its lifetime share price has grown 4.32% since inception.
