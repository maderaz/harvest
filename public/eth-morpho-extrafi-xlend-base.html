About ETH Extrafi XLend

ETH Extrafi XLend is a autocompounder vault on Base that accepts ETH deposits and routes them into the Morpho strategy. The vault automatically harvests rewards, swaps them back into ETH and redeposits, compounding returns over time without manual harvesting or restaking.
