About USDC Aerodrome

USDC Aerodrome is a autocompounder vault on Base that accepts USDC deposits and routes them into the Aerodrome - Base strategy. The vault automatically harvests rewards, swaps them back into USDC and redeposits, compounding returns over time without manual harvesting or restaking.
