About USDC Yearn OG V2

USDC Yearn OG V2 is a autocompounder vault on Base that accepts USDC deposits and routes them into the Morpho strategy. The vault automatically harvests rewards, swaps them back into USDC and redeposits, compounding returns over time without manual harvesting or restaking.
