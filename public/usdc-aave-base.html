About USDC Aave

USDC Aave is a autocompounder vault on Base that accepts USDC deposits and routes them into the Aave - Base strategy. The vault automatically harvests rewards, swaps them back into USDC and redeposits, compounding returns over time without manual harvesting or restaking.

The vault currently holds $28 in deposits and is generating 3.27% APY over the last 24 hours. The 30-day average APY sits at 3.27%, and over its lifetime share price has grown 6.53% since inception.
