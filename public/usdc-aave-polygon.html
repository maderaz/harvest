About USDC Aave

USDC Aave is a autocompounder vault on Polygon that accepts USDC deposits and routes them into the Aave - Polygon strategy. The vault automatically harvests rewards, swaps them back into USDC and redeposits, compounding returns over time without manual harvesting or restaking.

The vault currently holds $467 in deposits and is generating 2.61% APY over the last 24 hours. The 30-day average APY sits at 2.61%, and over its lifetime share price has grown 7.85% since inception.
