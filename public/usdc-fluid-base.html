About USDC Fluid

USDC Fluid is a autocompounder vault on Base that accepts USDC deposits and routes them into the Fluid strategy. The vault automatically harvests rewards, swaps them back into USDC and redeposits, compounding returns over time without manual harvesting or restaking.
