About USDC Fluid

USDC Fluid is a autocompounder vault on Base that accepts USDC deposits and routes them into the Fluid - Base strategy. The vault automatically harvests rewards, swaps them back into USDC and redeposits, compounding returns over time without manual harvesting or restaking.

The vault currently holds $123K in deposits and is generating 10.30% APY over the last 24 hours. The 30-day average APY sits at 9.25%, and over its lifetime share price has grown 26.19% since inception.
